# BBJF Eye-Catching Design Patch v8

This patch makes the BBJF UI visually stronger and more unique without using busy photo backgrounds behind forms.

## Highlights

- Bolder BBJF red/white/green accent ribbon.
- More visible aurora/gradient page backgrounds.
- Larger and more premium home hero design.
- Rotated photo slider card with floating chips.
- Mini step cards in home hero.
- Login/signup glass cards with top brand stripe.
- Stronger dashboard and registration backgrounds.
- Form readability remains safe.
